Injection Speed Test

How to Test

  1. Open devtools
  2. Reload page
  3. Make sure that script and scriptlet rules were injected at least before the inline script at the end.

⚠️ Note: In the case of a cold start of Safari Web Extension (i.e. AdGuard for iOS or AdGuard Mini for macOS) injection time may be longer and thus the rules will be executed later.
